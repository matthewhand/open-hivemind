## 2024-05-18 - Missing ARIA Labels on Configuration Actions
**Learning:** This app frequently relies on the `LockClosedIcon`, `PencilSquareIcon`, and `TrashIcon` mapped to small ghost or square buttons for configuration entries. Many of these do not provide `aria-label` text by default. Screen readers will just announce these as "button" with no context regarding what configuration key or entity they affect.
**Action:** When adding or modifying edit/delete/status toggle controls mapped to icon-only components in this project, explicitly verify that `aria-label` properties describe *which* item is being altered (e.g. ``aria-label={`Edit ${item.name} profile`}``) instead of generic "Edit" to provide essential context.
